# Keyword Seeds for Restaurant SEO

Templates for Instagram keyword-first captions and hashtag seeds. Replace `{placeholders}` with values from `restaurant-profile.json`.

## High-Intent Keyword Phrases (Lead the Caption With These)

- `{cuisine} restaurant in {city}`
- `best {dish} in {city}`
- `{city} {neighborhood} {meal}` — "stockholm gamla stan lunch"
- `{cuisine} near {landmark}` — "italian near gamla stan"
- `{meal} {occasion} {city}` — "dinner date night stockholm"
- `where to eat {dish} in {city}`
- `{cuisine} with {distinctive feature} {city}` — "italian with fresh pasta stockholm"

## Content-Type Modifiers

| Content type | Keyword angle |
|---|---|
| Story-behind-dish | `{cuisine} tradition {city}`, `authentic {cuisine}` |
| Sourcing | `local {cuisine} {city}`, `farm-to-table {city}` |
| Chef POV | `{cuisine} chef {city}`, `{cuisine} kitchen {city}` |
| Promo | `{cuisine} happy hour {city}`, `{cuisine} prix fixe {city}` |
| Seasonal | `{season} menu {city}`, `{ingredient} season {city}` |
| Event | `{event name} {city}`, `tasting menu {city}` |

## Hashtag Seeds

### Hyper-specific (3–5 per post, high priority)

- `#{city}{cuisine}` — `#stockholmitalian`
- `#{neighborhood}{meal}` — `#gamlaStanDinner`
- `#{specificDish}{city}` — `#bolognesestockholm`
- `#{restaurantName}` — if established; skip for new accounts
- `#{cuisine}In{city}` — `#italianinstockholm`

### Mid-tail (3–5 per post)

- `#{cuisine}food`
- `#{cuisine}restaurant`
- `#{city}restaurants`
- `#{city}foodie`
- `#fresh{cuisine}`

### Never Use

- `#foodporn`, `#instafood`, `#foodie`, `#love`, `#instagood` — all >10M posts, your post is invisible.
- Irrelevant trending tags. The post needs to match the tag; forcing it tanks reach.

## Per-Cuisine Seed Lists

For the six most common cuisines we deploy to, quick seed hashtags:

### Italian
`#freshpasta`, `#handmadepasta`, `#italianauthentic`, `#trattoria`, `#italianwine`, `#italianchef`

### Japanese
`#omakase`, `#japaneseauthentic`, `#sushichef`, `#japanesekitchen`, `#izakaya`

### French
`#frenchbistro`, `#frenchcuisine`, `#frenchwine`, `#bistrot`, `#frenchchef`

### Nordic
`#nordiccuisine`, `#newnordic`, `#{country}food` (swedishfood, norwegianfood), `#localingredients`

### Mediterranean / Middle Eastern
`#mezze`, `#{specific} cuisine` (lebanese, turkish), `#oliveoil`, `#mediterraneandiet`

### Indian
`#authenticindian`, `#indiantandoor`, `#indianchef`, `#{region}indian` (keralaindian, punjabiindian)

For cuisines not listed, use `web_search` once at onboarding to pull current high-performing hashtags for that cuisine in that city.
