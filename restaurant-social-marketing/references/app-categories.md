# Restaurant Category Templates

## Italian / Mediterranean

**Slide concept:** Same table setting, same candlelit vibe, different dishes from the menu.

**Base prompt template:**
```
iPhone photo of [DISH] served on a white ceramic plate at a cozy Italian
restaurant. Rustic wooden table, red-and-white checkered napkin to the side.
Warm candlelight, exposed brick wall in background. Wine glass partially
visible at edge of frame. Natural phone camera quality. Portrait orientation.
```

**Dish progression for 6 slides:**
1. Antipasti / bruschetta (hook slide — visually striking starter)
2. Fresh pasta being twirled on a fork (build desire)
3. Main course plated (the discovery)
4. Close-up of sauce/texture detail (reaction)
5. Dessert or cheese board (escalation)
6. Full table spread or restaurant exterior (CTA)

## General Tips for All Categories

- **Steam sells.** If the dish is hot, show steam. It makes food look fresh and appetizing.
- **Sauce drips sell.** A little imperfection (sauce on the plate rim, a crumb) makes it look real.
- **Hands in frame** (holding fork, tearing bread) make shots feel personal and less stock-photo.
- **Lock the background.** The setting should feel like ONE restaurant visit, not six different places.
- **Portrait always.** 1024x1536. Fills the TikTok screen.
- **Warm lighting.** Cool/clinical lighting makes food look unappetizing. Always warm.
