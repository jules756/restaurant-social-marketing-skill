# 🍕 Restaurant Social Media Marketing Skill - Simple Installation Guide

## 📋 What This Skill Does
This skill helps restaurants automatically create and post food content on TikTok, Instagram, and Facebook to get more customers. It:
- Makes tasty-looking food photos using AI
- Writes engaging text on the images
- Posts to your social media accounts
- Learns what works to get more bookings
- Handles special offers and promotions automatically

## 🚀 How to Install This Skill (Simple Steps)

### Step 1: Get the Skill Content
1. Go to: https://github.com/jules756/restaurant-social-marketing-skill
2. Click on "SKILL.md" 
3. Click the "Raw" button (top right of the file view)
4. Copy ALL the text you see (from the first line to the last line)

### Step 2: Install in Your Hermes Agent
In your Hermes agent chat, type this command EXACTLY:

```
skill_manage create --name restaurant-social-marketing --content "PASTE_ALL_THE_TEXT_YOU_COPIED_HERE"
```

**Important**: 
- Replace `PASS_ALL_THE_TEXT_YOU_COPIED_HERE` with the actual text you copied from GitHub
- Keep the quotes at the beginning and end
- Make sure you copied ALL the text from the SKILL.md file

### Step 3: Wait for Installation
The Hermes agent will:
1. Show "Creating skill..." 
2. Then say "Successfully created skill: restaurant-social-marketing"
3. Immediately start chatting with you to set things up

## 🔧 What Happens During Setup (Simple Flow)

### First: Language Setup
The agent will ask in English:
1. "What language do you want to US to speak in?" (e.g., Swedish, English)
2. "What language should we POST in on social media?" (can be different)

### Second: Tech Check (Automatic)
The agent will CHECK (not ask you):
- Is your TikTok connected? 
- Is your Instagram connected?
- Is your Facebook connected?
- Can it access your Google Drive photos?

If something isn't connected, it will tell you exactly how to fix it.

### Third: Restaurant Confirmation
The agent will:
1. Look at your Google Drive folder
2. Say: "I see this looks like [Restaurant Name]. Is that right?"
3. You say Yes or correct it

### Fourth: Learn About Your Restaurant
Now the real conversation starts! The agent will ask questions ONE AT A TIME:
- "What's your restaurant called and what food do you serve?"
- Based on your answer: "What makes your place special?"
- Based on your answer: "Tell me about your signature dishes"
- And so on... building up knowledge about YOUR specific restaurant

## 📱 How to Use It After Setup

### Daily Process:
1. **Every morning**: The agent creates food post drafts and puts them in your TikTok inbox
2. **You review**: Check the drafts in your TikTok inbox
3. **You publish**: Add a trending TikTok sound and hit publish (takes 30 seconds)
4. **Agent learns**: Tracks which posts get more bookings and makes better ones tomorrow

### Special Features:
- **Promotions**: If you tell the agent "We have 20% off pasta this week", it automatically makes promo posts
- **Booking tracking**: Connects to your booking system to see what posts actually get customers
- **Improvement**: Gets smarter over time about what content works best for YOUR restaurant

## 🛠️ Troubleshooting Common Issues

### "I don't see drafts in my TikTok inbox"
- Make sure you published the previous day's drafts
- Check that your TikTok is still connected in Composio
- The agent creates drafts around 11am, 5pm, and 8:30pm your time

### "The food photos look weird"
- Tell the agent during setup what you DON'T like
- It will adjust the prompts and regenerate
- You can also use your REAL photos from Google Drive instead of AI-generated

### "I want to post more/less often"
- The agent makes drafts 3x daily (optimal times for food decisions)
- You choose WHEN to publish from the drafts
- Publish 1, 2, or all 3 drafts each day - it's up to you

## 💡 Pro Tips for Best Results

1. **Be specific** when describing your restaurant - the more details, the better the content
2. **Mention everything** that makes you special: family recipes, local ingredients, special techniques
3. **Use the promotion feature** - just tell the agent about specials and it handles the rest
4. **Check your daily report** - the agent sends you a summary each morning of what worked
5. **Be patient** - it takes about a week of posting to really learn what works for YOUR audience
